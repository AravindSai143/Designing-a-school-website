Annual Events & Celebrations

Students and staff participate in a school-wide yoga session promoting wellness, mindfulness, and healthy living.

Flag hoisting, cultural performances, and patriotic songs mark this important national holiday.

A heartwarming day where students express gratitude with performances, speeches, and fun games for teachers.

Christmas celebration with skits, carols, and decorations followed by winter holidays.

Celebrated with respect and honor through flag hoisting, parade, and cultural programs.
