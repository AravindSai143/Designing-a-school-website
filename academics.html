Our Curriculum

At Thrisharan E/M School, we aim to build strong foundations for the future of every student. Our curriculum is carefully designed to foster academic excellence, creativity, and holistic development from Grades 1 to 10. We focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical applications, preparing students for future academic pursuits and life challenges.

Primary and Middle School (Grades 1-8)

English Language & Literature

A comprehensive English program that enhances students' reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. The curriculum includes storytelling, grammar, and creative writing.

Mathematics

From basic arithmetic to geometry and algebra, we offer a structured approach to math, ensuring students grasp essential concepts for advanced learning.

Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Hands-on experiments and interactive lessons make science an exciting subject. Students learn the fundamentals of physics, chemistry, and biology, preparing them for high school.

Social Studies

Our social studies curriculum includes history, geography, and civics, encouraging students to explore their world, understand cultures, and learn about global events.

ICT & Computer Science

With technology becoming central to education, we teach basic computer skills, coding, and digital literacy to foster creativity and problem-solving.

High School (Grades 9-10)

Advanced Mathematics

At the high school level, students engage with algebra, geometry, and basic calculus. We encourage logical thinking and problem-solving through real-world applications.

Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology

Our science curriculum offers in-depth knowledge in all three subjects. Students perform advanced experiments, conduct research, and gain a deeper understanding of scientific concepts.

Languages (Hindi/Regional Language)

A key component of our curriculum is language proficiency. We offer Hindi and regional languages to build communication skills and cultural awareness.

Social Science & Civics

Students explore history, geography, economics, and politics, preparing them for the real world with a strong understanding of societal structures.

Art, Music & Physical Education

Creativity is nurtured through art and music, while physical education promotes fitness and wellness. We believe in a balanced development of both the mind and body.
